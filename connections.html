The 10 Connection Types

Every relationship between two soul types falls into one of ten structural categories. Each category describes a distinct dynamic, with its own quality of pull, its own recurring patterns, and its own particular gifts and costs. These are not judgments. They are maps.

⚡
Frequency Twins
The Complete Reflection
Every layer of one soul perfectly complements the other. The rarest and most frictionless dynamic in the system.
Tension level: 5%
🌟
Twin Stars
Deep Complementarity and Shared Resonance
Two layers in perfect complement, two in shared resonance. A bond that completes and mirrors simultaneously.
Tension level: 15%
✨
Cosmic Flow
The Natural Rhythm
A sustainable current of mutual support. These souls generate energy for each other without conscious effort.
Tension level: 25%
🪞
Mirror Portals
Inspiration with an Edge
Strong pull and genuine recognition alongside a persistent gap. Inspiring, but always carrying an edge.
Tension level: 35%
📚
Celestial Mentor
The Teaching Dynamic
Real gifts alongside real friction. One soul holds natural authority in areas where the other is still learning.
Tension level: 50%
🔗
Karmic Bonds
The Magnetic Return
Enough connection to keep drawing them together, enough friction to make it consistently unresolved.
Tension level: 60%
🌓
Shadow Contracts
The Activating Mirror
These relationships surface what is unresolved. The activation is real, and so is the difficulty.
Tension level: 70%
🕳
Black Holes
The Intense Depletion
Enormous charge, structural depletion. The pull is intense because the unmet need is so strongly activated.
Tension level: 80%
⚖️
Star Alchemy
The Crucible of Transformation
Maximum structural opposition. These are the relationships that fundamentally change who you are.
Tension level: 95%
♾
Eternal Reflection
The Perfect Mirror
The same soul type meeting itself. Total recognition, zero friction, and no expansion through difference.
Tension level: 0%
